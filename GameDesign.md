# Game Design

## Premise
You are a mod developer who sits infront of a computer doing various tasks in order to create a mod for your favorite game.

## Story/Narrative
You decide to create a mod for the game "Gamer Warriors". Once you start working on the mod however, your room catches on fire without any option for you to escape. After some smoke inhalation and some hallucinating, modding becomes a real world process (such as using your trusty bug spray to fix bugs). Your goal is to finish the mod before the fire finishes you.

## Timeline
1. You first start off by creating a project in the provided modding software.
2. Room catches on fire.
3. Smoke inhalation & hallucination reveals a more immersive modding experience
4. Tutorial on mechanics
5. Creating the mod
6. Uploading/publishing the mod

## Goal
Finish & publish the mod (you are dead anyway)

## Main Character
Generic mod developer

## Environment
A basic bedroom with a gaming pc setup in the corner. The room is also on fire.
